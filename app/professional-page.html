4 PILLARS OF FORMULATION:

The chemistry may be complex, but the concept is simple: to give you the strongest, most effective tools to help your client’s skin look and feel its best. Our continued innovation and groundbreaking success comes from focused attention to our ingredient standards and four pillars of formulation

  • INTELLIGENT EXFOLIATION allows our active ingredients to penetrate the skin for maximum efficacy.
  • BOTANICAL INGREDIENTS create cleaner formulations.
  • SKIN MATRIX SUPPORT provides the building blocks for cell rejuvenation and skin restoration.
  • CHIRAL CORRECTION creates a partnership with the skin to reduce irritation ensuring desired results

THE POWER OF METABOLIC PEELS & HOME CARE:

We discovered a far more effective way to peel back rough, blemished and aging skin without harsh chemicals that can damage the skin, contribute to “inflammaging” and require extensive downtime. COSMEDIX’s revolutionary approach is found in our metabolic peels made of chirally correct botanical ingredients and potent actives. They tell the skin to peel by working holistically to help stimulate the skin’s natural renewal process instead of forcing it into submission. Our peels help repair the skin’s barrier function with the use of purified alpha hydroxy acids, proprietary encapsulated Retinol, Nobel Prize-winning chemistry as well as utilizing a complex blend of antioxidants and anti-inflammatory ingredients. This revolutionary approach helps you minimize shock to your client’s skin and the risk of adverse side effects. Complemented by COSMEDIX’s gentle yet effective home care, your clients can help prep, protect and maintain their results between treatments
