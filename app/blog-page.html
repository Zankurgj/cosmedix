Winner, Winner: National Aesthetician Month Winners Announced!

Celebrate National Aesthetician Month with COSMEDIX! Known as the highlight of our year, this month-long celebration lets us shine a light on the skincare professionals who help expertly transform the look of skin. What was once a one-day event on October 15th, has quickly taken the whole month by storm, with online contests, skincare awareness, educational events and more!

In an effort to recognize the heart and soul of the skincare industry, COSMEDIX holds two annual contests inviting clients and managers to nominate their favorite aesthetician. The first contest is held regionally and allows Account Executives to express their gratitude by nominating skincare professionals that have gone above and beyond. Winners get to relish in a boosted awareness for their practice and are awarded with the title ‘Aesthetician of the Year’ by COSMEDIX!

OCTOBER 9, 2020

Aesthy of the Week: Dakotah Tyrene Esthetics

In honor of National Aesthetician Month, we’re handing picking a skincare expert each week to dish on their favorite COSMEDIX skin care products, plus their secret tips and tricks for healthier-looking skin. Say hello to our first Aesthetician of the Week: Dakotah from Dakotah Tyrene Esthetics.

All About Dakotah

Dakotah Tyrene has been in the beauty industry for 7 years, specializing in acne prone skin types for the past 5 years. Her career started at a medical spa where she learned the ins and outs of skincare with clinical treatments, deep chemical peels and extensive training on cosmetic ingredients. Dakotah’s skincare career took off in 2018 when she was finally able to open her own business in downtown Kirkland, WA. She has a huge passion for sharing and spreading skincare knowledge, and she uses her Instagram as a channel to educate her amazing clients and people with similar interests!
